Story of Zhare

By Snape Dumbledore | March 17, 2025 | Date Soon to be Written

UNDER CONSTRUCTION

Continuation of Story of Zhare Book 1. From Chapter 12 to this new chapter and book.

Now that the multiverse is now reset or restored, but now at the new cycle, e.g., Cycle 104.

The focus of this story or chapter is on Prime 1 Earth 1.

This Earth in Prime 1 is exactly like the real-world nations, history, figures of interest, etc.—all according to real-world events in the past.

(The trick here is if an Earth or Plane is near 0 or its number is quite similar or exactly the same as real-world events, no changes occur whatsoever. The Earth Prime is exactly the same as Earth 1, with the only difference being its people, their births, and such, but history-wise, everything remains accurate. You may refer to Chapter 13 of Book 1 if you need to recall things.)

Our focus here is on a young man who is now on the verge of success. What I mean by that is he is almost done with his studies. It took him almost seven years.

Now, I know what you are asking: what is he studying, right? Well, he is studying Physics, Biology, and Chemistry—or should I say Biochemistry—Engineering, and, to some extent, Genetics and Robotics.

Now, that is indeed a lot of degrees.

You might be asking, who is this guy? His name is Rafael Zack. That is his first and second name but not his last name.

(As you know, we rarely put surnames in this story, except for Barry from the previous book. We gave him the name Barry Garick.)

This guy—Rafael Zack or RZ for short—is our guy for what is to come. Possibly the establishment of something greater than himself or all of the multiverse.

Now, the focus then shifts to Prime 1 Earth 10. Then, there was a scene of the sunrise on that Earth, e.g., Earth 10.

Then next was.

Rafael Zack, or RZ, graduated years later, in about his eighth year of college or university. He graduated with honors and was awarded some amazing certificates, e.g., Best in Thesis, Best in Robotics Engineering, and lastly, Best in Genetics and Genealogy.

Yes, this man was gifted. People saw this as a threat, a moron, a fool, or something that was not like them. Or perhaps, it was more than just what met the eye. This was indeed not a normal thing from a real-world standpoint, but geniuses are often depicted as mad scientists.

Thus, RZ, or Rafael Zack, never had acquaintances or friends, but he did have the support and love of his parents.

RZ, or Rafael Zack, officially graduated in March of 2024. Yet, despite applying, he was unsuccessful in getting a job, even though he received interviews one after the other. But none were successful.

But then.

After that.

Continuation of the “But then.”

RZ came into contact with something—not an illegal activity or such, but more of an item rather than an actual event. He came across an item far more powerful than anything else in the universe.

Thus, he later found out that this stone was an all-around stone—a combination of a wishing stone, the elixir of life, or something akin to the Sorcerer’s Stone (or Philosopher's Stone) from HP, and a power stone. As long as he could hold onto this stone, he would be powerful, invincible, or something beyond comprehension.

He made some wishes, and these were (to simplify them): Money, Machinery (state-of-the-art), and Protection.

Context of These Wishes:

  • Money -
    Not to be rich or anything, but just to be able to survive in a world where money is needed. As a wish, this money couldn’t be used by anyone unless it was given willingly by him. If not, there would be a curse upon those who tried to use it.
  • Machinery -
    Another term for Pre-Fab Inventions. Below is a list of inventions that fall under this category.

List of Pre-Fab Inventions:

  1. Genesis Chamber -
    A human creation chamber using DNA.
  2. Animal Chamber -
    Similar to the Genesis Chamber but for animals.
  3. Robotics Chamber -
    Creates robots (like in Terminator or I, Robot) but without AI.
  4. Farogen Technology -
    An energy source using water, similar to a dam but non-combustible.
  5. Arc Reactor -
    A technology similar to Iron Man's Arc Reactor.
  6. Miniaturized Factories -
    Factories that produce miniature items, like toy car-sized products.
  7. Enlargen -
    A machine that enlarges miniaturized items to real-life size.
  8. Quantum Locker -
    A storage system for mass production with capacities of up to 999 units.
  9. Accelerator -
    Speeds up the growth of humans and animals in the Genesis and Animal Chambers.
  10. Replicator -
    Creates solid resources with liquid inside.
  11. Synthesizer -
    Creates purely liquid-based resources without a container.
  12. Duplicator -
    Duplicates only solid resources.
  13. Energy Source -
    Powers weaponry or shielding technology (e.g., Plasma Railgun, Plasmatic Energy Shield).
  14. Vibranium-like Resource -
    A rare ore, similar to vibranium from the Black Panther movie but not exactly the same.
  15. Foundry -
    Creates items.
  16. Assembly -
    Assembles products into packages or containers.
  17. Dyson Sphere -
    A small-scale energy production system.
  • Protection -
    Shields against all external threats incoming to the user. It functions as both an active and passive shield. However, it does not protect against self-inflicted harm.

Thus, that is that.

Now back to the story.

Rafael Zack had been thinking:

  • “What can I do with these?”
  • “Can I really be this powerful?”
  • “Will this stone make me immortal, like in HP (Harry Potter)?”
  • “What can I do with these?”
  • “Who will I become with this power?”
  • And many other thoughts.

Thus, that was it for RZ, or Rafael Zack. His own thinking was what was stopping him from achieving his own greatness.

After that.

RZ, or Rafael Zack’s parents (both his mother and father), were hospitalized and declared “dead on arrival.” This incident was due to a car accident.

On the other hand, Rafael Zack took his parents to a funeral home and buried them. As per their wishes, RZ followed their instructions regarding the funeral, burial, and other matters, including their last will and testament.

Now at home, RZ began activating one of the machines. In fact, he turned on almost all of them.

RZ started creating clones—siblings like a brother and sister—and even some of his own unique creations, such as:

  • Mermaids
  • Centaurs
  • Satyrs
  • Minotaurs
  • Fauns
  • Werewolves
  • Vampires
  • Dragons (with the ability to transform between human and dragon form)

Thus, he continued creating more and more, equipping them with weapons for their use:

  • Mermaids -
    Tridents
  • Centaurs -
    Spears
  • Satyrs & Fauns -
    Flutes
  • Minotaurs -
    Hammers
  • Dragons (in human form) -
    Spears
  • Werewolves & Vampires -
    No weapons

Abilities:

  • >Mermaids -
    Water abilities
  • Dragons -
    Fire abilities
  • Centaurs, Satyrs, Fauns, and Minotaurs -
    No special powers but naturally strong

Thus, RZ was now preparing to build a powerful, armed, and enhanced force.
