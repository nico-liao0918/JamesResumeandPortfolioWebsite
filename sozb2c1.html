Story of Zhare

By Snape Dumbledore | March 17, 2025 | Date Soon to be Written

UNDER CONSTRUCTION

Continuation of Story of Zhare Book 1. From Chapter 12 to this new chapter and book.

Now that the multiverse is now reset or restored, but now at the new cycle, e.g., Cycle 104.

The focus of this story or chapter is on Prime 1 Earth 1.

This Earth in Prime 1 is exactly like the real-world nations, history, figures of interest, etc.—all according to real-world events in the past.

(The trick here is if an Earth or Plane is near 0 or its number is quite similar or exactly the same as real-world events, no changes occur whatsoever. The Earth Prime is exactly the same as Earth 1, with the only difference being its people, their births, and such, but history-wise, everything remains accurate. You may refer to Chapter 13 of Book 1 if you need to recall things.)

Our focus here is on a young man who is now on the verge of success. What I mean by that is he is almost done with his studies. It took him almost seven years.

Now, I know what you are asking: what is he studying, right? Well, he is studying Physics, Biology, and Chemistry—or should I say Biochemistry—Engineering, and, to some extent, Genetics and Robotics.

Now, that is indeed a lot of degrees.

You might be asking, who is this guy? His name is Rafael Zack. That is his first and second name but not his last name.

(As you know, we rarely put surnames in this story, except for Barry from the previous book. We gave him the name Barry Garick.)

This guy—Rafael Zack or RZ for short—is our guy for what is to come. Possibly the establishment of something greater than himself or all of the multiverse.

Now, the focus then shifts to Prime 1 Earth 10. Then, there was a scene of the sunrise on that Earth, e.g., Earth 10.

Then next was.

Rafael Zack, or RZ, graduated years later, in about his eighth year of college or university. He graduated with honors and was awarded some amazing certificates, e.g., Best in Thesis, Best in Robotics Engineering, and lastly, Best in Genetics and Genealogy.

Yes, this man was gifted. People saw this as a threat, a moron, a fool, or something that was not like them. Or perhaps, it was more than just what met the eye. This was indeed not a normal thing from a real-world standpoint, but geniuses are often depicted as mad scientists.

Thus, RZ, or Rafael Zack, never had acquaintances or friends, but he did have the support and love of his parents.

RZ, or Rafael Zack, officially graduated in March of 2024. Yet, despite applying, he was unsuccessful in getting a job, even though he received interviews one after the other. But none were successful.

But then.

After that.

Continuation of the “But then.”

RZ came into contact with something—not an illegal activity or such, but more of an item rather than an actual event. He came across an item far more powerful than anything else in the universe.

Thus, he later found out that this stone was an all-around stone—a combination of a wishing stone, the elixir of life, or something akin to the Sorcerer’s Stone (or Philosopher's Stone) from HP, and a power stone. As long as he could hold onto this stone, he would be powerful, invincible, or something beyond comprehension.

He made some wishes, and these were (to simplify them): Money, Machinery (state-of-the-art), and Protection.

Context of These Wishes:

  • Money -
    Not to be rich or anything, but just to be able to survive in a world where money is needed. As a wish, this money couldn’t be used by anyone unless it was given willingly by him. If not, there would be a curse upon those who tried to use it.
  • Machinery -
    Another term for Pre-Fab Inventions. Below is a list of inventions that fall under this category.

List of Pre-Fab Inventions:

  1. Genesis Chamber -
    A human creation chamber using DNA.
  2. Animal Chamber -
    Similar to the Genesis Chamber but for animals.
  3. Robotics Chamber -
    Creates robots (like in Terminator or I, Robot) but without AI.
  4. Farogen Technology -
    An energy source using water, similar to a dam but non-combustible.
  5. Arc Reactor -
    A technology similar to Iron Man's Arc Reactor.
  6. Miniaturized Factories -
    Factories that produce miniature items, like toy car-sized products.
  7. Enlargen -
    A machine that enlarges miniaturized items to real-life size.
  8. Quantum Locker -
    A storage system for mass production with capacities of up to 999 units.
  9. Accelerator -
    Speeds up the growth of humans and animals in the Genesis and Animal Chambers.
  10. Replicator -
    Creates solid resources with liquid inside.
  11. Synthesizer -
    Creates purely liquid-based resources without a container.
  12. Duplicator -
    Duplicates only solid resources.
  13. Energy Source -
    Powers weaponry or shielding technology (e.g., Plasma Railgun, Plasmatic Energy Shield).
  14. Vibranium-like Resource -
    A rare ore, similar to vibranium from the Black Panther movie but not exactly the same.
  15. Foundry -
    Creates items.
  16. Assembly -
    Assembles products into packages or containers.
  17. Dyson Sphere -
    A small-scale energy production system.
  • Protection -
    Shields against all external threats incoming to the user. It functions as both an active and passive shield. However, it does not protect against self-inflicted harm.

Thus, that is that.

Now back to the story.

Rafael Zack had been thinking:

  • “What can I do with these?”
  • “Can I really be this powerful?”
  • “Will this stone make me immortal, like in HP (Harry Potter)?”
  • “What can I do with these?”
  • “Who will I become with this power?”
  • And many other thoughts.

Thus, that was it for RZ, or Rafael Zack. His own thinking was what was stopping him from achieving his own greatness.

After that.

RZ, or Rafael Zack’s parents (both his mother and father), were hospitalized and declared “dead on arrival.” This incident was due to a car accident.

On the other hand, Rafael Zack took his parents to a funeral home and buried them. As per their wishes, RZ followed their instructions regarding the funeral, burial, and other matters, including their last will and testament.

Now at home, RZ began activating one of the machines. In fact, he turned on almost all of them.

RZ started creating clones—siblings like a brother and sister—and even some of his own unique creations, such as:

  • Mermaids
  • Centaurs
  • Satyrs
  • Minotaurs
  • Fauns
  • Werewolves
  • Vampires
  • Dragons (with the ability to transform between human and dragon form)

Thus, he continued creating more and more, equipping them with weapons for their use:

  • Mermaids -
    Tridents
  • Centaurs -
    Spears
  • Satyrs & Fauns -
    Flutes
  • Minotaurs -
    Hammers
  • Dragons (in human form) -
    Spears
  • Werewolves & Vampires -
    No weapons

Abilities:

  • >Mermaids -
    Water abilities
  • Dragons -
    Fire abilities
  • Centaurs, Satyrs, Fauns, and Minotaurs -
    No special powers but naturally strong

Thus, RZ was now preparing to build a powerful, armed, and enhanced force.

After that.

Rafael Zack had now managed to buy an island in the Philippines. (Also, RZ lives in the Philippines—I may have forgotten to mention that.)

This island is off the coast of Palawan, near the contested waters but still owned by the Philippines. However, this island is not a natural landmass. It is St. Thomas Shoal, and the ship aground there was purchased by RZ from the Philippine government.

The Centaurs, Satyrs, Fauns, Werewolves, Vampires, and Minotaurs had now suited up, allowing them to appear as if they were walking or running on water. Their suits had several advanced features, including the ability to walk on water, holster weapons, and much more.

Meanwhile, the Mermaids had waterproof suits capable of withstanding underwater pressure, similar to the diving suits in the game Subnautica. However, these suits had everything they needed— protection from extreme cold and heat, weapon holsters, and additional enhancements.

The Dragons could simply transform into their dragon form, but in human form, they also had specialized suits similar to the rest of RZ’s creations.

These creations were not like traditional mythical creatures:

  • Mermaids -
    Unlike traditional mermaids, they did not have tails.
  • Werewolves -
    They had no traditional weaknesses but were giant wolves or wolf-like creatures, similar to those in Twilight.
  • Vampires -
    They also had no weaknesses but needed to feed on blood. However, they could control their thirst and function normally.

The other species retained their traditional appearances and abilities:

  • Minotaurs -
    Half-bull, half-human.
  • Satyrs & Fauns -
    Half-goat, half-human.
  • Dragonkind -
    Humans with the ability to transform into dragons.

The Dragons were depicted much like those in classical dragon- slaying myths—massive, four-legged creatures with full wings and the ability to breathe fire.

Thus, that is how RZ, or Rafael Zack, accomplished these things.

After that.

Rafael Zack gave a command to his Mermaids to stay in the waters near the “island,” while the rest remained on the “island,” awaiting further orders from RZ.

Suddenly, the Mermaids spotted something unusual and reported back to RZ.

Ellie, Queen of Mermaids, "Sir Rafael, we spotted a ship, but it does not belong to any of the nearby nations. It has some kind of unfamiliar characters or script. We also took a picture of it."

Ellie handed over the camera to RZ.

RZ, "Thank you, Ellie."

RZ examined the picture and immediately recognized the characters— it contained Chinese script. The Chinese navy had been spotted somewhere near the 12 NM boundary of St. Thomas Shoal.

Without hesitation, RZ ordered Ellie to use force if the ship crossed within 10 NM of the island or St. Thomas Shoal.

(For those who may not know, NM stands for Nautical Miles. This is just for informational purposes, not to imply that readers are unaware.)

Meanwhile, on the "island," RZ set up the first-ever Mini Factories (a shorthand for "Miniaturized Factories," item number 6 from the Pre-Fab Inventions list). These factories immediately began mass- producing essential resources, including food, water, firearms, ammunition, shells, and other supplies.

Thus, that was what RZ did following the sighting of the incoming Chinese vessel.

After that.

Rafael Zack issued a warning to the incoming Chinese vessel with this message:

Rafael Zack, "Greetings, China Ship,

I am Rafael Zack of St. Thomas Shoal Island, formerly of the Philippines.

However, this is no longer their domain.

It is mine.

Please do not enter my territorial waters, or else.

Regards, Rafael Zack."

The Chinese vessel did not respond yet, but it continued its approach toward the "island."

The Mermaids were positioned at the 10 NM boundary from the island's radius, fully prepared for any confrontation.

Thus far.

After that.

Rafael Zack saw through his binoculars that the ship had now entered the 10 NM (Nautical Miles) boundary. He immediately radioed Ellie and her Mermaids to attack.

This is how it went.

Ellie and her Mermaids peeked out of the water, their eyes just inches above the surface, and saw the ship approaching fast. As it passed, Ellie used her water abilities to freeze the rudder and propeller, rendering them immobile. At the same time, one of Ellie’s Mermaids summoned powerful waves, crashing them into the vessel. A giant wave, as large as the ship’s hull, struck the vessel before receding into the ocean. Another wave followed. The Chinese crew noticed the abnormality—there was no storm or any natural cause for such massive waves.

Then, later on, Ellie and her Mermaids, armed with spears and tridents, commenced boarding the ship from its hull and upper decks.

They created a whirlpool that lifted them up to the ship’s upper decks, making their presence visible. Using their abilities, they forced the bodies of the Chinese crew to stop moving. The Mermaids manipulated the water within their bodies, rendering them paralyzed. The soldiers, unable to resist, were left defenseless as the Mermaids unholstered their weapons, aimed at their heads, and pulled the trigger.

The Chinese soldiers were dead.

Moving throughout the ship, the Mermaids repeated the process until only a few remained in the Captain’s Deck or Bridge. They then forced the remaining crew, including the captain, to steer the ship away from St. Thomas Shoal. Once the task was complete, the Mermaids returned to the deck, dove into the ocean, and disappeared without a trace.

The Chinese vessel was now 15 NM away from the island, officially out of St. Thomas Shoal.

After that.

Now, RZ or Rafael Zack had succeeded in defending his own territories.

China immediately pressured the Philippines to take action against what they considered a declaration of war. However, before any official response could be made, RZ broadcasted a live message across every screen, radio, and audio-visual device worldwide.

Rafael Zack, "Greetings, world leaders. I am Rafael Zack of the Philippines. I have bought this island, St. Thomas Shoal, for a reason you leaders refuse to acknowledge as the truth."

Rafael Zack, "I bought this island to defend my homeland from invaders, oppressors, and tyrants like China."

(At this moment, the live feed displayed footage and images of the recent events—China’s intrusion and the counterattack.)

"This victory over China proves that nations can stand up against a superpower. You can stand and defend your ground!"

"But all you do is wait and talk, as if talking alone solves problems. You must act upon your words; only then will you not be liars and frauds."

"This is my island now. However, I will keep the Philippine flag with me as a sign of my homeland."

"Now, if I ever decide to wage war against you, China, I will do everything in my power to defeat you."

"You are nothing compared to what I am capable of."

Regards,
Rafael Zack
St. Thomas Shoal

This bold declaration from RZ sent shockwaves across the world.
