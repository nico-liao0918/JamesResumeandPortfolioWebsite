Chapter 2: Greatest Conquerors

Continuation of Chapter 1.

In Zhare’s Capital;

Both Huete and Noran set aside their differences and now join forces against this multiversal threat from Earth 666 and 879. King Noran of Dartha and King Huete of Hassar readied their standing troops in the hangar of Zhare’s capital city—Solace, which is technically in the Philippines.

However, the Philippines attacked Zhare across all its territories but were forced to retreat due to massive missile bombardments and artillery fire on Philippine forces. The President of the Philippines then asked Zhare for a ceasefire but was ignored. The King of Zhare commanded his forces—legions—to attack the P hilippine forces. The Zharian forces emerged victorious in battle.

The Philippines were forced to surrender and lay down their arms, thus waving the white flag. The President issued a plea for peace against Zhare, and Zhare responded with this statement:

King of Zhare, "Greetings, brethren in the Philippines, You have declared war against me but were unsuccessful in defeating us. This is just the strength of the Inner Kingdoms and not the entire Alliance and Coalition of Allied Earths.

I accept your surrender. But you shall be enslaved by us. As for you, Sulu Sultanate, I shall return to you Palawan and the ARMM Region of the Philippines. I see that you will indeed play a role in the future with us.

Hope that you, Sulu Sultanate, will help us in the far or near future.

And we shall help you whenever you, Sulu Sultanate, need it.

Regards,
King of Zhare"

And thus, the Philippines and Sulu responded with agreement. The treaty was signed for peace.

The discussion was as follows:

Philippines, "Thank you, Zhare. I apologize."

Zhare, "That is fine."

Sulu, "What will I do with these lands?"

Zhare, "Use them to your heart's content."

Sulu, "Can I apply for kingdomhood?"

Zhare, "Of course. Will you be able to fight alongside us?"

Sulu, "Yes."

Philippines, "What will I do now?"

Zhare, "I will grant your provinces kingdomhood. Examples of these are the Kingdom of Laguna, the Kingdom of Cavite, let's say."

Philippines, "I understand. I have been absolved."

Zhare, "Not entirely. You have been repurposed to become something more than just a country."

Philippines, "Like what?"

Zhare, "To become more than you ever know."

Sulu, "A kingdom?"

Zhare, "Yes, and the resources we have are unlimited. So, you will never go hungry ever again, and possibly even more."

Sulu, "I agree. I want to join."

Zhare, "Of course, in due time, Sulu. But as for your provinces, Philippines, they will undergo harsh training and development first before each province is granted kingdomhood."

Sulu, "Thank you!"

Philippines, "Thank you. I understand."

End of discussion.

Now, on Earth 1, the satellites of the ArcNet began to boot up, now covering most parts of the world. They engaged their plasma railguns and plasma shields in preparation for danger approaching from outer space. A Fargone ship was now inbound. Remember what a Fargone is? Yes, a space-faring airship, according to Zharian terminology.

The satellites fully engaged their shields and weaponry. The Fargone ships had now entered Earth's outer orbit. Their origin and intent were unknown, but the satellites were in place as a deterrent. Zharian spaceships and the Fargone ships had now entered the area of interest.

The Zharian Fargone ship opened a channel and said;

Admiral Zod, "This is Earth. What is your intent?"

The unknown Fargone ship replied,

Chief of the Dar, "We don't come here to make war. We come in peace. We are the people of Navi from Pandora in Alpha Centauri. Please don’t attack."

Chief of the Dar, "We need help back on our planet. We have enemies that we need to fight off. Please help? I implore you to come to Pandora."

Admiral Zod, "We will in due time. Please land in our capital for food, water, and fuel. We also implore you to rest up before our journey to Pandora."

And thus, the Admiral commanded the ArcNet to stand down, and it did. The Pandoran Fargone ships have now safely landed on Zharian soil and are now under negotiations, possibly for technological and military agreements.

The discussion was as follows:

Zhare, "Hi."

Navi, "Hi, we need help."

Zhare, "We know. Calm down, we are still building our Fargone ships. For now, rest and enjoy the city."

Navi, "Thank you."

End of discussion.

Meanwhile, on Pandora:

T he enemy, whom we will denote as Nova (not in terms of Marvel, but still), has now taken control of most parts of Pandora and is mining its resources, including its valuable Fargone shipbuilding facilities. The Nova Nation comes from another planet within the same solar system as Pandora. They invaded Pandora for its resources and workforce, intending to plunder and exploit the planet for their own shipbuilding needs.

More and more ships are seen moving inbound and outbound between Nova and Pandora.

Back to Earth:

The Fargone ships are now almost complete, with a total of 300 Fargone ships constructed and nearly ready for launch. The Navi and Zhare have negotiated further expansion and base- building agreements, similar to the VFA agreement between the Philippines and America—only this time, between the Navi and Zhare.

Zhare has now ordered the launch of its Fargone ships into the air and space.

As Zhare is now fully prepared, all Fargone ships are airborne and in space, ready to jump to Pandora.

Admiral Zod, "All units, listen up. Turn the portal telemetry to coordinates MW SN 3. How many planets do you have, Chief?"

Chief, "Nine, sir."

Admiral Zod, "Input coordinates MW SN 3 9."

Staff, "Roger that. Inputting now. Initiating portal. Portal is now active."

Admiral Zod, "All units, enter the portal now!"

All ships, "Roger that, Admiral Zod."

Admiral Zod, "You too, Navi Fargone ships. Enter now."

Navi, "Roger, Admiral Zod."

Admiral Zod, "Now let's enter too. Full speed!"

Staff, "Roger."

As they entered Pandora's space, each of the 300 and 1 Fargone ships joined the fray.

The Nova Fargone ships had secured Pandora's space and now engaged their weapons, warning the incoming Fargone ships to stay away or face destruction.

But the Navi Fargone ships and the Zharian Fargone ships replied,

Admiral Zod, "This is Admiral Zod of the Zharian Nation. Stand down, Nova, or we will open fire."

Chieftain Naiivatu, "You have taken our homes. Now we fight for it. All Navi, let us take back our homes!"

On Pandora, all Navi had been gearing up and were now fighting the Nova forces on the planet, while the Zharian and Navi Fargone ships engaged the Nova fleet in space. Weapons were fired, and the battle erupted.

A Navi Fargone ship was damaged in the fight but managed to retreat. Zhare, on the other hand, not only defeated a single Nova Fargone ship but destroyed approximately 135 enemy Fargone ships in battle.

On the ground level, the Navi forces were fighting fiercely, with explosions erupting throughout the battlefield. The battle raged on and on until the final Nova Fargone ships fled Pandora's space, declaring,

Nova Forces, "This is not over. We will return stronger!"

And with that, they left Pandora's space.

Though the war had been won, uncertainty loomed over future invasions that might come. The Navi Fargone ships and two Zharian Fargone ships landed in Iviaana, the capital city of the Navi on Pandora.

The Pandorans spoke, "Zhare, thank you for helping us. We welcome you to our planet."

Zhare, "Thank you for your hospitality. We will leave a few ships in your space so that no one will ever invade you again."

Pandora, "Thank you."

Zhare boarded their ships and departed.

The Zharian Fargone ships also left Pandora's space, but 20 Fargone ships remained behind to protect Pandora under the leadership of Admiral Karin.

They opened a portal and left, now heading back to Earth.

Back on Earth. The Zharian coding of the ArcNet has now included Zharian Spaceships and Fargones, Navi Fargone Ships, Earth 2 Spaceships, Earth 3 Spaceships, and Earth 10 Spaceships into the system of ArcNet as allies and friendly forces. So the ArcNet won’t react to their ships when they come to Earth or Earth 1.

And the return of the victors from Pandora has come.They repaired any damage if there was any and have now landed in the hangar in the capital.

Zhare initiated a speech and said,

Zhare, "Greetings all, We are victorious in Pandora and have left some security for them in the future. We are now a powerful nation, both multiversal and universal. We intend to keep this alliance between Zhare and Pandora. As they need it more than we do. The invaders of Nova are a significant threat in the known universe. But we will handle them as soon as we can.

Regards.
King of Zhare
Capital Solace."

End of Speech.

So thus, the Zharian got to work in building more Fargone ships and their classes.Zhare was able to build Destroyer Class, Frigate Class, Aircraft Carrier Class, Missile Carrier Class, Mobile Command and Troop Carrier Class—Fargone ships, similar to their spaceships and navy ships.

Zhare has built almost 1,000 of each of these classes in a span of six years.

Zhare has now launched their fleet into space and is now awaiting orders.

Now, what remains of the victors in the Pandora fleet, plus these new additions, is now a total of 5,280 Fargone ships prepared to be commanded.

Admiral Zod took the captain's seat and said to all ships, "Engines online, let's prepare for Operation Naiita."

All ships said, "Copy that."

All ships opened their own portals to MW SN 3 9, entering and exiting them swiftly. As they entered Pandoran Space, they did not stop until they reached Nova Planet.

Admiral Zod spoke in an open channel to Nova Planet.

Admiral Zod, "This is Admiral Zod. We are here to negotiate. We don’t want you to take Pandora. Tell us what you want?

Regards,
Admiral Zod
Zharian Admiral
F253."

The Novans replied,

Novans, "Hmm. Tempting offer, but no! Tell us, what can you offer in return?"

Admiral Zod replied,

Admiral Zod, "We will provide resources, food, water—anything for you to overcome other nations in the galaxy. We are not here to conquer you. We are here to impose peace, even though we have our entire fleet here."

The Novans replied,

Novans, "Fine. Come to our capital, and let’s discuss further this so- called peace!"

Admiral Zod replied, "Understood. We will land shortly."

A few ships landed—two Mobile Command and Troop Carrier Class Fargone ships—onto Nova in their capital, Estika.

As the two Mobile Command and Troop Carrier Class Fargone ships landed, they were greeted by Novans who opposed the Zharians, but they were shielded by Novan Guards as they entered the palace.

The Zharians and Novans sat down and began negotiations for peace.

Zhare, "What do you need to stop attacking Pandora?"

Nova, "Resources rich in iron and steel for our forces."

Zhare, "What else?"

Nova, "That is about it."

Zhare, "Are you willing to wait for our ships?"

Nova, "What ships?"

Zhare, "Transport Fargone ships carrying the resources you need."

Nova, "Ahh, if you put it that way, I might budge. Hmm... What about Pandora?"

Zhare, "It is under our protection."

Nova, "I want to come with you to Pandora."

Zhare, "Okay, but not with your ships."

Nova, "Got it."

Zhare and Nova boarded the Mobile Command and Troop Carrier Fargone ships, launching into the air and space. They were now heading toward Pandora.

As both ships entered space, they left their army back at Nova. The Mobile Command and Troop Carrier went straight to Pandora, successfully entering orbit. An announcement was made on an open channel.

Admiral Zod, "Greetings all, I am Admiral Zod, and with me is the leader of Nova. I know you may have your differences, but hear him out."

(Admiral Zod hands the mic to the King of Nova.)

King of Nova, "Greetings, Pandorans. I greatly apologize for the conquest and for forcing you to mine and build our fleets. But I do hope we can work together in peace. I sincerely hope for that. No harm done, no grudges. We Novans are proud of what we can accomplish. But Zhare told us that we can have the resources we need even without conflict. I once saw you as enemies, but now I see you as allies in the Alpha Centauri system.

I hope we can work together! Even if my people hate this alliance, I still hope we can work things out.

Regards,
King of Nova
Nova"

Pandora replied;

Pandora, "Fine! You are cleared to land if you want. We have something for you. We finished constructing your ships and stocked them with resources for you. The Zharian fleet stationed above Pandora mentioned this to us in advance. You can claim them if you want."

End of Talks.

Meanwhile, back in Nova

The people of Nova gathered at the palace and announced;

Novan Dissenter, "Our leaders have betrayed us! They are joining forces with our enemies. Let us reclaim Pandora and destroy the threats in our own space!"

They all boarded their ships, launched into space, and opened fire on all Zharian ships. However, the Zharian fleet held their defensive positions and remained untouched.

The Zharian Fargone ships radioed back to the Mobile Command and Troop Carrier, calling them to return to Nova, but there was no response. Admiral Hon took command and ordered all ships to attack the hostile Fargone ships and destroy them immediately.

Admiral Hon reported back to Admiral Zod regarding the incident.

As the Mobile Command and Troop Carrier-class Fargone ships entered Novan space, accompanied by some Navi-operated Fargone ships for turnover to Nova, they witnessed the ongoing battle but remained silent.

King of Nova, "I did not authorize this."

Zharian and Navi Forces, "We know."

King of Nova, "You are cleared to open fire, Navi and Zhare."

Zhare and Navi Forces, "Got it."

They opened fire on the remaining Novan dissenter forces, destroying them completely.

Now, with the Novan King back in orbit, he asked Zhare to hold steady while he made an announcement via the open channel and said,

King Gregor, "I am King Gregor of Nova, of royal descent. I have heard of your attempt to overthrow me and your rebellion. I will not tolerate insurrection on my lands, even if you are loyal followers of the Novan Core Principles. I suggest the rest of the population stand down or be destroyed."

Novans, "Got it, we apologize, Your Majesty. I have retaken the palace from the rebels and have ensured a death penalty sentence onto them. I am Commander Nita."

King Gregor, "Thank you, Commander Nita."

End of the discussion.

Now, the two Mobile Command and Troop Carrier ships landed once again, and King Gregor entered his palace, where he was greeted by Commander Nita.

Nita, "Sir!"

Gregor, "At ease. Nice to see you, Nita."

Nita, "Yes, thank you."

Gregor, "How is our kingdom?"

Nita, "We have already arrested 14,000 dissenters across our kingdom and conducted planetary-wide arrests."

Gregor, "Good. Impose the death penalty on them as well. Let the people witness these executions so they will understand our true power."

Nita, "Understood."

Gregor, "And also, prepare rooms for our guests. They will stay in the palace."

Nita, "Understood, Your Majesty."

The Zharian delegates stayed in the palace overnight, then packed up and left, leaving behind a note.

Note:

"Sorry, we have to leave early. Our job here is done. I left a few ships in your space for any other dissenters that may arise.

Good luck, and have a good and pleasant day ahead—I know you will need it.

Also,

Your transport Fargone ships are now inbound and will be arriving in 14 days.

Thank you.
Regards,
Zhare Delegates."

King Gregor thought to himself, "This is great news."

King Gregor, "Let's prepare a landing zone for the inbound transport ships."

Commander Nita, "Understood."

Now, only a few ships remained in Nova Space—about 17 Fargone ships— while the rest left the space, entered Earth's atmosphere, and landed safely in the hangar.

Zharian Fargone ships were also stationed in Earth's orbit to guard against any future threats that might arise.

The King of Zhare then made another speech:

King James, "We have successfully gained another ally in the Zhare-Pandora-Nova alliance.

With this, we position ourselves as protectors of peace.

We also plan to integrate Pandora and Nova into our kingdom as soon as Nova’s dissenters are subdued. Pandora will soon become part of Zhare as a kingdom.

We will now send emissaries to both planets as soon as possible to grant them kingdomhood."

Regards,
King James
Zhare
Earth"

End of Speech

Now, the emissary forces were sent to Pandora and Nova for the meantime.

Zhare continued to build more Fargone ships and spaceships to enhance its fleets.

Additionally, Zhare sent emissaries to Earth 2, Earth 3, and Earth 10, requesting their full integration into Earth 1. The Kingdoms of Decka, Filus, and Hans were asked to join Zhare on Earth 1, which meant leaving behind their respective Earths.

Earth 2, Earth 3, and Earth 10 accepted the request and were now undergoing a massive exodus operation.

Now to Pandora:

The Pandorans accepted kingdomhood under one condition—they required Zhare to protect the entire solar system from external threats. The emissary agreed to the terms, and the delegation began their journey home.

Now to Nova:

The Novans accepted kingdomhood but requested additional ground security to ensure stability under the leadership of King Gregor. The emissary agreed, and they too began their return home.

Meanwhile, even the kingdoms of Hassar and Dartha from Earth 45 had initiated a massive exodus operation. They were leaving not only their own Earths but also the occupied territories, relocating everyone to Earth 1 under Zharian authority.

Now, with the massive exodus operations completed across the multiverse, the affected Earths—primarily Earths 2, 3, 10, 44, 45, and 46—were now abandoned. Earths 44 and 46 had been conquered by Noran from Dartha on Earth 45, prompting further exodus protocols.

Zhare, on the other hand, had prepared a ballistic missile capable of inter-multiversal travel, equipped with a Deconia Bomb.

With permission granted by the leaders of Decka, Dartha, Filus, Hans, and Hassar, the Deconia Missile was authorized for launch to eradicate Earths 2, 3, 10, 44, 45, and 46 once the exodus operations had concluded.

As soon as the final departures were confirmed, Zhare prepared to launch the ballistic missile toward the now-empty Earths, ensuring their absolute destruction.

Now shifting focus to another quadrant of the galaxy—

In a distant sector, possibly orbiting a red giant star, an unknown planet had emerged as a formidable superpower. This world, potentially Krypton, had begun establishing military bases, outposts, and colonies across the surrounding planets.

For now, this development would remain undisturbed.

Now, the Exodus Operation for Earths 2, 3, and 10 was complete. Zhare launched its ballistic missile, armed with the Deconia Bomb, toward these Earths.

The missile ascended into the air and activated a portal leading to the respective Earths. Upon arrival, the Deconia Bomb detonated, completely obliterating those planets.

With the operation concluded, Zhare’s ArcNet reactivated, bringing its weapons and shields online, including the Plasma Railgun and Plasma Shield Array.

Suddenly, Kryptonian Fargone ships were detected inbound. Their signal was traced from Krypton, located in the far quadrant of the Milky Way Galaxy.

The Kryptonian fleet halted at the upper orbit of Earth, staying beyond the range of Zhare’s ArcNet Defense Satellite. However, Zharian Fargone ships, along with newly designed space-worthy fighters, quickly engaged their weapons, preparing for combat.

The Kryptonians then transmitted an open-channel message:

General Kamra, "Greetings, Earth.
We have come here for your resources and to establish a colony.However, we must terraform your planet to accommodate our own people.
Surrender Earth to us.

Regards,
General Kamra
Krypton"

Admiral Zod, "No! This planet is under our protection and my rule. You don't stand a chance!
We will not surrender Earth to you!

Regards,
Admiral Zod
Zhare"

However, the Kryptonian fleet remained undeterred:

General Kamra, "I do not care. Whether you are a protector or not is irrelevant. I will initiate Protocol Terraform.

Regards,
General Kamra
Krypton"

At that moment, another fleet entered the fray—this time from Mars.

Martian Fargone Fleet, "This is the Martian Fargone Fleet, Nagonia.
